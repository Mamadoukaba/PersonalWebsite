ChillApp!

ChillApp is an place finder tool that help you find places to hangout. With only a word description and a distance slider, find places within selected distance that suit you best. After selecting and clicking your preferred option of the list, it's Foursquare page will be displayed, where you can check out awesome pictures and helpful reviews about the places. Plus you can also send the address of the place to a friend in your address book simply with the click of a button.
